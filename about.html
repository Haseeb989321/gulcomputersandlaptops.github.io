About Gul Computers

Your trusted partner in technology solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Gul Computers and Laptops started as a small computer repair shop with a vision to provide quality technology solutions to our community. Over the years, we've grown into a premier destination for computers, laptops, and accessories, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

What began as a one-person operation has now expanded to a team of 15 dedicated professionals, all committed to helping you find the perfect tech solutions for your needs.

We pride ourselves on our honest advice, competitive pricing, and exceptional after-sales support that keeps our customers coming back year after year.
